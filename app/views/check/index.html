Information for employers on coronavirus

User your discretion around the need for medical evidence if an employee is advised to stay at home because of coronavirus.

Employees who cannot work because of coronavirus should avoid going to their GP or a hospital to request a fit note. This is to let doctors focus on their patients.

If you need evidence to confirm that an employee cannot work because of coronavirus, ask them to get an isolation note.

Isolation notes are for employees who cannot work because they have symptoms of coronavirus or because they live with someone who has symptoms of coronavirus.

Working from home

To prevent the spread of coronavirus, you should encourage your employees to work at home, wherever possible.

If your employees can work from home, and are well enough to work, they do not need an isolation note.
