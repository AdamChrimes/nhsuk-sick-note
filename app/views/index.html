Get an isolation note

Use this service if:

you have to stay at home for more than 7 days because of coronavirus and:

  • you need a note for your employer

You do not need a note if you have to stay at home for 7 days or less. Instead your employer can ask you to confirm why you've been off. This is called self-certification.

Information:

If you have to stay at home but feel well enough to work, ask your employer if you can work from home. If you can work from home, you will not need an isolation note.

You can use this service on behalf of someone else.

Start now

Before you start

If you are not sure if you need to stay at home, get advice now from the 111 coronavirus service.
