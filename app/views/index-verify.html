Check an isolation note

This is a new service for employers.

It is not yet live but will be available soon.

Employers will be able to verify any isolation notes given to them by employees.

What are isolation notes?

An isolation note is an official confirmation of absence from work during the coronavirus outbreak.

The note confirms that the person has been told to stay at home by NHS 111, a medical professional or on official advice.
