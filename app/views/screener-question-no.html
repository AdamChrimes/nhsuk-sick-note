You can only get an isolation note if you have been told to stay at home

We can only give you an isolation note if you have been told to stay at home by NHS 111, a medical professional or official advice.

If you are not sure if you need to stay at home, get advice from:

  • England: NHS 111 online coronavirus service
  • Scotland: NHS inform
  • Wales: NHS Direct Wales
  • Northern Ireland: Call 111
